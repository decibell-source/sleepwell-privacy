FAQ

Common questions

Does it drain my battery?

SleepWell uses only the microphone and accelerometer — no GPS, no screen. Average consumption is 8-12% over a full night. We recommend plugging in your phone.

Is my microphone recording all night?

The microphone listens continuously for audio events, but nothing is sent anywhere. All processing is 100% local. Audio clips are only saved when snoring, bruxism, or sleep talk is detected — and you can delete them anytime.

Does it work without a smartwatch?

Yes. All 6 detectors work with just your phone. A Wear OS watch adds heart rate data for better apnea detection and sleep stage estimation, but it's entirely optional.

How accurate is the snoring detection?

SleepWell combines FFT spectral analysis with Google's YAMNet neural network to verify detections. This dual approach filters out false positives like traffic noise or a partner's movements.

Can it replace a medical sleep study?

No. SleepWell is a screening and tracking tool, not a medical device. If the app or the STOP-BANG test suggests sleep apnea risk, consult a healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis.

What's the difference between Free and Premium?

Free gives you full detection (snoring, position, smart alarm, sleep score). Premium unlocks audio recordings, detailed apnea analysis, bruxism & sleep talk insights, "Who Snores?" identification, weekly coaching, and data export.

Where should I place my phone?

On your nightstand, within 1 meter of your head. Face down is recommended to save battery. The microphone picks up sounds clearly from this distance.

Can two people use the app at the same time?

Yes! The "Who Snores?" feature uses AI to identify which partner is snoring. After 3 nights of learning, it attributes each snoring episode to the right person.
