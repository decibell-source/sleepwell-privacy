Important Health Warnings

  • Sleep apnea detection — The App uses audio analysis and optional heart rate data to estimate potential apnea events. These results are not a diagnosis. Only a qualified healthcare professional using polysomnography (PSG) or an approved home sleep test (HST) can diagnose sleep apnea.
  • STOP-BANG questionnaire — The STOP-BANG screening tool included in the App is for informational purposes only. A high score does not confirm sleep apnea. Consult a sleep specialist for proper evaluation.
  • Bruxism and sleep talk detection — These features provide estimates based on audio patterns. They are not diagnostic tools and should not replace evaluation by a dentist or sleep specialist.
  • Sleep scores and readiness scores — These are algorithmic estimates based on available data. They do not measure clinical sleep quality and should not be used to make medical decisions.
  • Sleep stage estimation — Sleep stages shown in the App are approximations. Accurate sleep staging requires clinical polysomnography with EEG monitoring.

If you suspect you have a sleep disorder, experience excessive daytime sleepiness, loud snoring with observed pauses in breathing, or any other concerning symptoms, consult a healthcare professional immediately.

Do Not Use This App As a Substitute For

  • Professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment
  • Emergency medical services — call your local emergency number for medical emergencies
  • Prescribed CPAP, oral appliance, or other sleep apnea treatment
  • Clinical sleep studies (polysomnography)
  • Regular check-ups with your healthcare provider

Never disregard professional medical advice or delay seeking treatment because of information provided by SleepWell.
