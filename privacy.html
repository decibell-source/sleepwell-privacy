Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Our Privacy Promise

100% Local Storage — All your sleep data stays on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on external servers. Your sleep is your business.

Data We Collect

Data Type Purpose Storage
Sleep sessions Track sleep duration and quality Local only
Audio recordings Detect snoring episodes Local only
Snoring events Analyze sleep quality Local only
Alarm settings Wake you up at the right time Local only
App preferences Personalize your experience Local only

Data We Never Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers for tracking
  • Browsing history
  • Contact lists
  • Photos or files outside the app

Microphone Permission

SleepWell requires microphone access to detect snoring and sleep sounds. Here's how we handle audio:

  • Real-time analysis only — Audio is processed in real-time to detect snoring patterns
  • Optional recording — Short audio clips of snoring episodes can be saved locally for your review
  • Never transmitted — Audio never leaves your device
  • You control deletion — Delete recordings anytime from Settings

Notification Permission

We use notifications for:

  • Alarm alerts to wake you up
  • Bedtime reminders (if enabled)
  • Sleep monitoring status

Notifications are processed entirely on your device.

Health Connect Integration

If you choose to connect SleepWell with Health Connect:

  • Sleep data can be shared with Health Connect on your device
  • This sharing is optional and controlled by you
  • Data stays within the Android Health Connect ecosystem
  • We do not have access to data from other Health Connect apps

Data Export (GDPR)

In compliance with GDPR and privacy regulations, you can:

  • Export all your data — Download a complete JSON file of all your sleep data from Settings
  • Delete your data — Clear all recordings and history from Settings
  • No account required — Since we don't collect data, there's no account to delete

Children's Privacy

SleepWell is designed for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since all data stays on-device, parents maintain full control over the app and its data.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top
  • Notify users through the app if changes are significant
  • Never change our core commitment to local-only data storage

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or SleepWell:

Email: privacy@joinsleepwell.com

Summary

SleepWell is built with privacy as a core principle. Your sleep data is yours alone. We don't have servers collecting your information, we don't sell data, and we never will. Sleep peacefully knowing your privacy is protected.
